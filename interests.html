The Birth of Venus

The Birth of Venus is a painting by Sandro Botticelli generally thought to have been made in the mid 1480s.

  • Artist: Sandro Botticelli
  • Dimensions: 5′ 8″ x 9′ 2″
  • Location: Uffizi Gallery
  • Created: 1484–1486
  • Periods: Renaissance, Italian Renaissance, Early renaissance, Florentine painting
  • Media: Paint, Tempera
