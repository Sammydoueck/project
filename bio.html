Guernica

Guernica is a mural-sized oil painting on canvas by Spanish artist Pablo Picasso completed in June 1937.

  • Artist: Pablo Picasso
  • Dimensions: 11′ 6″ x 25′ 6″
  • Location: Museo Nacional Centro de Arte Reina Sofía
  • Genre: Narrative art
  • Period: Surrealism
  • Created: April 26, 1937–June 1937
